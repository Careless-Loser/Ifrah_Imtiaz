I'm Ifrah Imtiaz, a first-year Creative Computing scholar with a strong background in
design, technology, and cybersecurity. I travel the field of creative computing
with a profound appreciation for innovation, fusing innovative ideas with state-of-the-art
tools to pave new avenues for investigation.

My career into the design field has been characterized by a steadfast dedication
to creating visually striking identities and a quest of aesthetic excellence.
I'm always looking for new ways to push the frontiers of creativity,
whether it's through digital artwork or envisioning inventive concepts.

Simultaneously, I'm fascinated by technology, especially in the field of cybersecurity,
where I'm essential to maintaining strong digital defenses.
I am a specialist in network security and information assurance, having worked
with Cisco and ISC2. I use my knowledge to protect digital assets from ever-changing dangers.

I have a passion for learning and a holistic approach to problem-solving,
and I am committed to making significant contributions to the creative
and technological domains. As I set out on this exploratory voyage,
I can't wait to uncover new information, question the existing quo,
and, in the end, make a significant contribution to the field of creative computing.
